An interactive story of an organism's life.

Play as Sundura from birth to death in a habitat rich of life. Upon birth you will be raised by your mother, migrating your way north through routes established by your ancestors. All the while embracing what it means to be alive as you learn to survive, play and collaborate with the life around you, and eventually give birth to new life of your own. But unlike the lives of your ancestors, your life will be effected by a new growing force of artificial organisms that are slowly extracting the life out of your habitat.
