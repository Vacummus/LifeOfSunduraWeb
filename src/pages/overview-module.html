An interactive story of an organism's lifespan, from birth to death.

Sundura is an organism that is constantly on the move, having to migrate with her family north to the feeding grounds and then back south to mating/birthing grounds. Hey species manuevarbility brings balance to the habitat which allows life to flourish. Sundura is an organism who is born into a habitat that is slowly diminishing due to the extraction of vital resources by the ever growing artificial organisms. Upon birth she must adapt inorder to unite with her mother.
