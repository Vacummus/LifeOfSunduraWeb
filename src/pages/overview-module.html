An interactive story of an organism's life.

Play as Sundura from birth to death in a habitat that is slowly diminishing due to the extraction of vital resources by the ever growing artificial organisms.

Inspiration

Much of the game is inspired by the conflicts between nature and industrilization. Through out the industrilization era, forests have been cut down, landscapes have been reshaped, rivers have been polluted, etc. From the perspecive of a deer or a bird that lives in a forest, seeing these gigantic machinery that cut down trees must be very unnatural to the organic life style that they are used to. And it is this perspective that is at the heart of the game's inspiration.
